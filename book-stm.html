Sovereignty through mathematics

Have you ever thought about the nature of money and exchange between people? Or why some things are called money and why some are better than others? Or why one can't just save the result of their work in national currency without it loosing its value with time? If so, I believe you'll find Knut Svanholm's book "Sovereignty through mathematics" interesting and inspiring. It touches on the most pressing problems of modern society, both economic and social, and introduces a newcomer to Bitcoin.
